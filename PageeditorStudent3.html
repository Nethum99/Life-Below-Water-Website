User Profile

The project at wrist is creating a user profile page that has progressive prompts to gradually gather user data. In order to create a thorough user profile, the procedure leads users through several types of prompts, including tasks, qualifications, and personal information. When users submit information, the system dynamically updates and displays the user profile, adding more details gradually based on the users' replies. Basic information is first provided. Users may track their progress from initial completion to 100% by receiving feedback on their profile completion status. Users are free to choose what information they provide, and they can choose to skip instructions or steps and come back to finish them at a later time. This process assures a fascinating and straightforward experience for users to constantly form and enhance their profiles.
